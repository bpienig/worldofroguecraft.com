10. Turn right as the passage opens into a larger room and there will be a set of stairs going down to the RIGHT (left stairs go to general)

11. The next area is a bit like frogger. There are gnomes running back and forth in front of you. Avoid them and turn right at the end of the big room, before you get to the next big room.

16. Stealth passed the Golem that is guarding the broken door. Once you�re through the door head down the stairs and across the open area to the other door.
